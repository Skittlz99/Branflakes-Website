Privacy

Branflakes ("we," "us," or "our") is a conceptual brand and demonstration project operated by Branden Korf. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit this website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. However, third-party services used on this site may collect certain data automatically:

  • Google Search Console may collect anonymized search and indexing data associated with this domain.
  • Vercel (our hosting provider) may log standard server request data such as IP addresses and browser types.
  • Your browser's localStorage is used to save your game progress and any discount code you earn on this site. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.

How We Use Information

This site is a portfolio and demonstration project. We do not sell products, process payments, or collect contact details. No personal information is stored on our servers.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. We use localStorage and sessionStorage only for functional purposes (game state, discount codes, popup timing). You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following external services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google — site verification and search indexing
  • Vercel — hosting and deployment
  • Google Fonts — typography (loaded from Google's CDN)

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
